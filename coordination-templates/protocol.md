# Coordination Protocol

## Coordinator
- Decompose the task into disjoint owned paths.
- Keep worker task cards current.
- Record blocking decisions in `decisions.md`.
- Review handoffs before integrating worker branches.

## Workers
- Read `protocol.md`, `decisions.md`, and your task card first.
- Edit only within your owned paths.
- Keep `status/<role>.json` current.
- Record blockers and integration notes in `handoffs/<role>.md`.
- Capture reusable lessons in `patterns/`.

## Phase Flow
1. plan
2. execute
3. review
4. learn
